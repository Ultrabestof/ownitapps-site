Medical information is among the most sensitive data your family produces. Medical Binder OS keeps it on your device — not a company's server.

## Built for Real Family Life

Doctor visits, ER trips, new specialists, prescription changes, school immunization forms — family health records scatter across providers, portals, and paper. Medical Binder OS gives you one organized place you control.

Add a profile for each family member. Log their medications with full dosage and prescriber details. Record allergies with reaction type. Track every immunization with the date and provider. When you need it — at 2 AM in an emergency — it is there.

## The Emergency Info Card

Every family member gets a printable one-page emergency card:
- Blood type
- Critical allergies (with severity)
- Current medications and dosages
- Primary doctor and phone number
- Emergency contacts
- Insurance member ID

Print one for your wallet. Send one with your kids to camp. Give one to a caregiver. This card alone is worth the $19.

## Why Not Just Use MyChart or a Patient Portal?

Patient portals are provider-specific. They do not talk to each other. They expire when you change insurers. They are locked behind logins. And the data is in a format the hospital controls, not you.

Medical Binder OS is your personal copy — portable, private, and always accessible.
