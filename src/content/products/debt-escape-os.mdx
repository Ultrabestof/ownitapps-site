Debt Escape OS turns the complexity of debt payoff into a clear, visual system — offline, private, and yours forever.

## How It Works

Open the HTML file in your browser. Add your debts: credit cards, student loans, car payments, medical bills. Debt Escape OS calculates your minimum payment schedule, then shows you exactly what happens when you put extra money toward the debt.

Choose your strategy:
- **Snowball**: smallest balance first — fast psychological wins
- **Avalanche**: highest interest rate first — least total interest paid

The payoff timeline updates in real time. You see exactly which month you become debt-free.

## Why Offline Matters for Debt Tracking

Debt data is sensitive. Account numbers, balances, creditor names, payment histories — this is not data you want living on someone else's server. Debt Escape OS keeps everything in a local browser file on your machine. No sync. No upload. No breach risk.

## The Interest Cost Breakdown

Most people underestimate what debt costs. The interest cost view shows you:
- How much total interest you will pay at the minimum rate
- How much you save by paying extra each month
- The exact dollar difference between snowball and avalanche for your specific debts

This is the number that motivates action.
