## What Is Paycheck-Based Budgeting?

Paycheck-based budgeting means you plan exactly how you'll spend your next paycheck *before* it arrives. Every dollar is assigned a purpose: rent, groceries, savings, entertainment. When your paycheck lands, you already have a plan.

Budget by Paycheck OS is built around this method. It keeps you disciplined without the anxiety of checking a live bank balance — because you already know where every dollar is going.

## How It Works

1. **Enter your paycheck** — Add income amount and expected date
2. **Schedule your bills** — Due dates, amounts, and categories
3. **Allocate remaining dollars** — Groceries, gas, savings, fun money
4. **Track as you spend** — Update each category as money goes out
5. **Export when done** — Download a CSV of your full budget cycle

## Why No Bank Connection?

Connecting a budgeting app to your bank account requires sharing banking credentials or authorizing third-party access to your financial data. Budget by Paycheck OS skips this entirely. You enter your own numbers. Your bank knows nothing about this tool. This is intentional: your financial data stays local and private.
