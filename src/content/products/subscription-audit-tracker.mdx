# Subscription Audit Tracker

Subscription Audit Tracker is built for people who want to review recurring charges without adding another recurring charge to manage them.

Most subscription trackers create a strange problem: you subscribe to software to monitor subscriptions. OwnitApps takes the opposite approach. This tool is designed as a private, offline-first tracker that helps you list payments, renewal dates, cancellation decisions, and review notes.

## What it helps you track

- Subscription name
- Monthly or annual cost
- Renewal date
- Payment method
- Keep, cancel, pause, or review status
- Cancellation notes
- Estimated yearly total

## Who it is for

This tool is useful for households, freelancers, students, and anyone who wants to reduce recurring financial leaks without connecting bank accounts or creating a cloud profile.

## OwnitApps philosophy

Buy once. Use privately. Keep your financial review data under your control.
