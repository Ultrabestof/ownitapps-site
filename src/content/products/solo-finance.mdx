Solo Finance is the first OwnitApps tool — built for freelancers who want clarity over their money without surrendering control to a subscription-based service.

## What Solo Finance Does

Solo Finance gives you a complete financial command center inside a single HTML file:

- **Dashboard**: See your revenue, expenses, profit, and tax liability at a glance with SVG charts
- **Transactions**: Log income and expenses with category, client, date, and notes
- **Clients**: Manage clients and attach transactions to each
- **Invoices**: Track invoice status — pending, paid, overdue
- **Tax Reporting**: See IRS-standard expense breakdowns and estimated quarterly taxes
- **Mileage Log**: Track business trips with automatic IRS rate calculation
- **Export**: Download your data as CSV or JSON any time

## How Your Data is Stored

Solo Finance uses the **File System Access API** as the primary storage method. When you open the file, it reads from and writes to a designated data file on your computer.

A **localStorage backup** automatically mirrors your data in the browser as a secondary layer.

**No data is ever sent to a server**. OwnitApps never receives, processes, or has access to your financial records.

## Who Solo Finance Is For

Solo Finance is designed for:
- Freelancers tracking income across multiple clients
- Self-employed individuals preparing for tax season
- Consultants who want clean records without QuickBooks pricing
- Anyone tired of Mint, YNAB, or Wave requiring accounts and subscriptions
- Privacy-conscious people who want financial records only on their own device

## What Solo Finance Is Not

Solo Finance is not a full accounting system. It does not:
- Sync with bank accounts
- Handle payroll or employees
- File taxes on your behalf
- Replace a CPA for complex tax situations

It is a focused, practical ledger for solo practitioners.
