## Data Ownership

Data ownership means users keep meaningful control over their records, exports, and files instead of being locked inside a rented account.

## Why it matters

Data ownership matters because access to your own records should not depend entirely on an active subscription or remote account.

## OwnitApps context

OwnitApps uses this concept to build practical tools that are offline-first, privacy-first, and designed around user ownership.
