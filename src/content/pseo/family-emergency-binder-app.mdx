## Why emergency information needs a system

In an emergency, families should not have to search through old texts, notebooks, emails, or cloud folders.

The most important information should already be organized, easy to review, and printable when needed.

## What a family emergency binder should include

A strong emergency binder should include:

- Emergency contacts
- Doctor and medical notes
- Allergy or medication notes
- Insurance policy details
- Utility and service provider contacts
- Household instructions
- Childcare or school information
- Pet care notes if relevant
- Caregiver or babysitter instructions
- Printable emergency summaries

## Digital plus printable is stronger

Paper is useful during emergencies, but paper gets outdated. Digital records are easier to update, search, and reorganize.

The strongest approach is digital-first with print-ready emergency pages.
