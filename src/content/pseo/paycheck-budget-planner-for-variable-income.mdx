# Paycheck Budget Planner for Variable Income

Variable income makes budgeting harder because the month does not arrive evenly.

A paycheck budget planner helps by assigning each incoming payment to specific jobs before spending begins.

## Why monthly budgets can fail

Monthly budgets often assume predictable income. That does not work well for freelancers, gig workers, commission earners, and households with irregular pay.

## What to track

Track expected income, confirmed income, fixed bills, savings priorities, debt payments, and flexible spending.
