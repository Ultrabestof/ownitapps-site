## Why home documents need more than folders

A cloud folder can store files, but it does not always help a family understand what those files mean.

A household may have insurance PDFs, warranty documents, repair receipts, school forms, medical notes, lease details, utility contacts, and emergency instructions spread across many places.

The real problem is not only storage. It is structure.

## What a private home document organizer should track

A useful home document organizer should help you track:

- Important document names
- Document categories
- Where each document is stored
- Who the document belongs to
- Renewal dates
- Provider contacts
- Policy or account notes
- Maintenance history
- Warranty details
- Emergency relevance
- Printable household summaries

## Start with the most important records

Families should usually organize these first:

1. Insurance policies
2. Emergency contacts
3. Home repair records
4. Warranty information
5. Utility providers
6. Medical notes
7. Lease or mortgage details
8. School or childcare documents
9. Pet records if relevant
10. Caregiver instructions

You do not need to organize everything at once. Start with the documents someone may need during a stressful moment.

## Why privacy matters

Home documents can reveal sensitive details about your household.

They may include names, addresses, policy numbers, medical notes, provider contacts, account information, routines, and emergency instructions.

A private local-first organizer helps keep the working system closer to your device instead of turning every household record into another cloud dependency.

## Home document organizer vs Google Drive

Google Drive is useful for file storage, but a document organizer solves a different problem.

A home document organizer helps answer:

- What is this document?
- Why does it matter?
- Who needs it?
- Where is the current version?
- When should it be reviewed?
- What household task does it support?
- Should this be printed for emergencies?

That structure is what makes the system useful.
