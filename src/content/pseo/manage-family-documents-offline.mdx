## Why family documents are different

Family documents are not casual notes. They can include medical details, insurance records, warranties, IDs, emergency contacts, school documents, and home maintenance history.

## Offline-first workflow

A good offline workflow keeps the working copy on your device, organizes records by household category, and adds a habit of regular manual backups.

## Recommended OwnitApps tool

Home Admin Binder OS is designed as a private household dashboard for warranties, service contacts, documents, and emergency information.
