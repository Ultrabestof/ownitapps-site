## Why households outgrow paper binders

Paper binders are familiar, but they become outdated quickly.

A phone number changes. A policy renews. A routine shifts. A repair happens. A new document arrives. If the binder is not updated, people stop trusting it.

A digital household binder makes updating easier.

## What to organize

A useful digital household binder can include:

- Important household contacts
- Emergency contacts
- Insurance information
- Medical notes
- Home maintenance logs
- Appliance and warranty details
- Family routines
- Home documents
- Provider lists
- Printable emergency summaries

## Why not just use a cloud folder?

A cloud folder stores files, but household admin is more than file storage.

Families also need structured information: who to call, what to do, when something was repaired, where a document belongs, and what a caregiver needs to know.
