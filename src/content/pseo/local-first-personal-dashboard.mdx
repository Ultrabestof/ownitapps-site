## What local-first means here

Local-first means your own device is the primary home for your records. Cloud services may be useful for optional backup, but they should not be required to access your own system.

## Why it matters

This model supports ownership, privacy, and resilience. If a service changes pricing, shuts down, or locks an account, your local records remain available.
