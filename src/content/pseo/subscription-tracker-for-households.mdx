# Subscription Tracker for Households

A subscription tracker for households helps families see recurring payments in one place.

Many households pay for streaming, storage, software, memberships, apps, insurance add-ons, and small digital services across multiple cards.

## Why household subscriptions become messy

The problem is not one subscription. The problem is the combined yearly total.

A few small recurring charges can turn into hundreds or thousands per year.

## What to review

Track who uses each service, how often it renews, which card pays for it, and whether the household still needs it.

Subscription Audit Tracker is designed for private recurring payment reviews without connecting financial accounts.
