# Private Debt Tracker for Couples

A private debt tracker for couples helps two people understand the same payoff plan.

It should make the debt picture clearer without turning personal finance into another cloud account.

## What couples should track

- Creditor
- Balance
- Interest rate
- Minimum payment
- Payoff strategy
- Notes and decisions
- Progress over time

## Why privacy matters

Debt data is sensitive. A local-first tracker can help couples review payoff plans without sending every detail into another platform.
